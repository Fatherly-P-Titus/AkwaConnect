Introduction

Akwa-Connect ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in compliance with Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019.

verified NDPR Compliance

Akwa-Connect fully complies with Nigeria Data Protection Regulation 2019. Fatherly P. Titus serves as the Data Protection Officer.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, date of birth, gender
  • Location Data: LGA (Local Government Area), city of residence
  • Profile Information: Hobbies, interests, preferences, bio
  • Relationship Preferences: Who you're looking to match with
  • Accessibility Information: Disability details (optional)
  • Photos: Profile pictures (if provided)

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Location Data: Approximate location (with permission)
  • Log Data: Access times, pages viewed, errors

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Other users (e.g., reports, interactions)
  • Social media (if you connect accounts)
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide personalized match recommendations
  • Enable communication between users
  • Improve our matching algorithm
  • Send notifications (with your consent)
  • Ensure Platform safety and security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Conduct research and analytics

Legal Basis for Processing (NDPR Compliant)

We process your data based on:

Consent:
You explicitly agree to data processing
Contract:
Processing necessary for service provision
Legitimate Interests:
Improving services while respecting your rights
Legal Obligation:
Compliance with Nigerian laws

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3.2 When We Share Information

We may share information:

  • With Other Users: Profile information as part of matching
  • With Service Providers: Hosting, database, analytics services
  • For Legal Reasons: When required by Nigerian law
  • During Business Transfers: Merger, acquisition, or sale
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

3.3 Third-Party Services

We use these trusted services:

Service Provider Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Database hosting User profiles, messages
Render Backend hosting API requests, usage data
GitHub Pages Frontend hosting Public website content

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) servers. We retain data:

  • While Account Active: For the duration of your account
  • After Deletion: 30-day grace period for recovery
  • Legal Requirements: As required by Nigerian law (up to 6 years)
  • Backup Copies: Archived backups for 90 days

5. Your Rights Under NDPR 2019

As a Nigerian data subject, you have the right to:

Right to Access:
Request a copy of your personal data (free of charge)
Right to Rectification:
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Right to Erasure:
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
Right to Restrict Processing:
Limit how we use your data
Right to Data Portability:
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
Right to Object:
Object to certain processing activities
Right to Withdraw Consent:
Withdraw consent at any time

How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact our Data Protection Officer:

  • Email: dpo@akwaconnect.com
  • Response Time: Within 7 business days
  • Verification: We may need to verify your identity
  • No Fee: Exercising rights is free (unless excessive)

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Authentication: Keeping you logged in
  • Security: Protecting against fraud
  • Preferences: Remembering your settings
  • Performance: Improving site speed

We do NOT use:

  • Advertising/tracking cookies
  • Third-party analytics without consent
  • Cross-site tracking

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security:

  • Encryption: HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Secure Authentication: Password hashing and salting
  • Access Controls: Limited access to sensitive data
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring
  • Backups: Regular encrypted backups

8. Children's Privacy

Akwa-Connect is for adults 18 years and older. We do not knowingly:

  • Collect data from children under 18
  • Market to children
  • Allow children to create accounts

If we discover a child's data, we will delete it immediately. Parents can contact us at dpo@akwaconnect.com.
