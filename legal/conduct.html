Introduction

Akwa-Connect is built on trust, respect, and authenticity. This Code of Conduct outlines expected behavior for all users. By joining our community, you agree to uphold these standards.

star Our Community Values

Respect • Honesty • Safety • Kindness • Cultural Pride

1. Respect and Decency

Treat every user with dignity and respect:

1.1 Communication Standards

  • Use appropriate, non-offensive language
  • Respect others' boundaries and preferences
  • No harassment, threats, or intimidation
  • Be patient and understanding
  • Accept rejection gracefully

1.2 Cultural Respect

  • Respect diverse backgrounds and beliefs
  • No discrimination based on ethnicity, religion, or disability
  • Celebrate Akwa Ibom cultural diversity
  • Be mindful of cultural sensitivities

2. Authenticity and Honesty

2.1 Profile Authenticity

  • Use real, recent photos (not heavily edited or filtered)
  • Provide accurate personal information
  • Don't impersonate others or use fake identities
  • Don't create multiple accounts
  • Be honest about your intentions

2.2 Prohibited Profile Practices

DO NOT:

  • Use photos of celebrities or models
  • Post photos that aren't you
  • Hide your face in all photos
  • Use group photos as your main picture
  • Post inappropriate or explicit photos

3. Safety Rules

security Golden Safety Rules

Your safety is your responsibility. Follow these guidelines:

3.1 Before Meeting

  • Video verify: Have a video call before meeting
  • Research: Look up your date online
  • Inform someone: Tell a friend/family member your plans
  • Share details: Provide meeting location and time
  • Set check-ins: Arrange to check in during the date

3.2 During Meetings

  • Meet in public: Coffee shops, restaurants, parks
  • Stay sober: Limit alcohol consumption
  • Transportation: Use your own transport or reliable services
  • Stay in public areas: Don't go to private locations immediately
  • Trust your instincts: If uncomfortable, leave immediately

3.3 Financial Safety

money_off Financial Safety Warning

  • NEVER send money to other users
  • NEVER share bank details or credit card information
  • NEVER invest with someone you met online
  • NEVER provide your login credentials
  • REPORT anyone asking for money

3.4 Online Safety

  • Protect personal info: Don't share home address too soon
  • Use Platform messaging: Keep communication on-platform initially
  • Be cautious with links: Don't click suspicious links
  • Report threats: Immediately report threatening behavior

4. Prohibited Content and Behavior

4.1 Strictly Prohibited

ZERO TOLERANCE FOR:

  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Explicit sexual content
  • Violent or threatening language
  • Fraudulent or scam behavior
  • Commercial solicitation
  • Illegal activities

4.2 Profile Content Rules

  • No nudity or sexually explicit content
  • No hate symbols or offensive imagery
  • No promotion of illegal substances
  • No contact information in bios
  • No spam or advertisements

5. Reporting System

5.1 How to Report

Report violations through:

  • Profile Report Button: Three dots on any profile
  • Message Report: Flag inappropriate messages
  • Email: report@akwaconnect.com
  • Emergency: Contact local authorities if in danger

5.2 What to Include in Reports

  • Screenshots of violations
  • Username of violator
  • Date and time of incident
  • Detailed description
  • Your contact information

5.3 Response Time

  • Urgent threats: Within 24 hours
  • General reports: Within 48 hours
  • Appeal responses: Within 7 business days

6. Consequences for Violations

6.1 Enforcement Actions

Violation Level Possible Actions Duration
Minor (first offense) Warning, content removal Immediate
Moderate (repeat) Temporary suspension 7-30 days
Severe (safety risk) Permanent ban, account deletion Permanent
Criminal activity Legal action, law enforcement report As needed

6.2 Appeal Process

If you believe you were wrongly penalized:

  1. Email appeal@akwaconnect.com
  2. Include your username and appeal reason
  3. Provide any supporting evidence
  4. We'll review within 7 business days
  5. Our decision is final

7. Cultural Sensitivity

Akwa-Connect celebrates Akwa Ibom culture. We encourage:

7.1 Cultural Appreciation

  • Respect for local traditions and customs
  • Celebration of cultural festivals and events
  • Understanding of family values
  • Appreciation of local cuisine and arts

7.2 Religious Respect

Akwa Ibom has diverse religious communities:

  • Respect all religious beliefs
  • No religious discrimination
  • Be mindful of religious sensitivities
  • Celebrate religious diversity

7.3 Language and Communication

  • English is the primary language
  • Local languages (Ibibio, Annang, etc.) are welcome
  • Be patient with language differences
  • Use respectful language at all times

8. Acceptance and Agreement

By creating an Akwa-Connect account, you:

  • Agree to follow this Code of Conduct
  • Accept responsibility for your actions
  • Commit to creating a safe community
  • Agree to report violations
  • Accept consequences for violations

check_circle Community Pledge

"I pledge to treat all members with respect, maintain honesty in my profile, prioritize safety in all interactions, and contribute positively to the Akwa-Connect community."

This Code of Conduct helps create a safe, respectful environment for all users.
